# **App Name**: Healthcare AI

## Core Features:

- Image Upload & Analysis: Allow users to upload images of skin conditions or medication tablets for AI analysis.
- AI-Driven Diagnosis: The AI will attempt to identify potential skin conditions based on image analysis.
- AI Tablet Scan: The AI will analyze medication images and list known benefits, risks and usage.
- Results Display: Clearly show AI analysis results, including potential conditions, remedies, and risks. The system will use a tool to decide whether to include additional relevant health info.
- Doctor Appointment: Provide an interface for users to book appointments with relevant specialists (this will be a placeholder for external links).
- Easy Image Upload: Make it very easy to upload images from various devices (PC, phone, tablet)
- Educational Content: Present helpful guidance about skin health/medication safety that can be read independently of the AI results.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm Blue (#77B5FE) to convey trust and health.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue (#EAF2FF) for a clean, clinical feel.
- Accent color: Soft green (#90EE90) to indicate positive or safe outcomes.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a modern and clean appearance; headline font: 'Space Grotesk', also sans-serif, for a techy but friendly appearance.
- Use simple, line-style icons for clarity and ease of understanding.
- Maintain a clean, structured layout for easy navigation and information retrieval.
- Use subtle animations during AI analysis to indicate progress and keep users engaged.
